Disaster Management

Preparedness, response, and recovery for community resilience

About Disaster Management Program

Our disaster management program focuses on building community resilience through comprehensive preparedness, rapid response, and effective recovery strategies. We work to minimize the impact of natural and man-made disasters on our community.

Disaster Management Activities

Period: Year-round preparedness & response
Coverage: Kaliganj Block & Emergency Areas

Preparedness

Community training, early warning systems, and emergency planning

Response

Rapid deployment of rescue teams and emergency relief operations

Recovery

Rehabilitation support and infrastructure restoration assistance

Mitigation

Risk assessment and vulnerability reduction programs
