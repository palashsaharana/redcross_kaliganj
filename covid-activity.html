COVID-19 Response Activities

Our dedicated efforts to combat the pandemic and support our community

Our COVID-19 Response

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Indian Red Cross Society Kaliganj Block Branch stepped up to provide critical support to our community. From March 2020 to November 2021, we conducted 15 major programs to ensure the safety and well-being of the people in our region.

Our dedicated volunteers worked tirelessly through lockdowns, managing quarantine centers, organizing blood donation camps, providing oxygen support, and facilitating vaccination programs to help our community navigate through these challenging times.

Key COVID-19 Activities

Our comprehensive response to the pandemic

Blood Donation Camps

Organized multiple blood donation camps during the pandemic, even during "Janata Curfew" to ensure blood supply.

  • 6 blood donation camps organized
  • 372 blood donors participated
  • AC Bus mobile blood donation units

Sanitization Program

Comprehensive sanitization of markets, government offices, hospitals, and residences of COVID patients.

  • Kaliganj Market & Ration Shops
  • Police Station & Hospital
  • Government offices sanitized

Quarantine Center Management

Managed Panighata Quarantine Center and COVID testing center sanitization for 3 months.

  • Full management of quarantine center
  • Sanitization of testing centers
  • 3 months continuous service

Oxygen Parlor

Established oxygen parlor with concentrators and cylinders during the critical oxygen shortage period.

  • 2 Oxygen Concentrators
  • 2 Handy Oxygen Cylinders
  • 2 months continuous operation

Vaccination Program Support

Facilitated vaccination programs at BDO office, Red Cross Bhavan, and Rural Hospital.

  • Debagram BDO office support
  • Red Cross Bhavan vaccination
  • Ongoing vaccination assistance

Medical Programs

Organized specialized medical camps including thalassemia detection and free health check-ups.

  • Thalassemia detection camp
  • Free medical check-up for 170 people
  • Blood sugar & ECG testing

COVID-19 Activities Timeline

Chronological overview of our pandemic response efforts

22/03/2020

Blood Donation Camp - Janata Curfew

Organized blood donation camp at Bhaga Primary School during "Janata Curfew" with 74 blood donors participating.

06/05/2020

Mother Care Program

Served dry and nutritious foods to expectant mothers of distressed families during the lockdown period.

08/05/2020

Sanitization Program Launch

Inaugurated comprehensive sanitization program covering Kaliganj Market, Ration Shop, Police Station, Hospital, and government offices.

15/05/2020

Quarantine Center Management

Took charge of Panighata Quarantine Center and COVID testing center (Pather Sathi) sanitization for 3 months.

01/10/2020

Mobile Blood Donation Camp

Organized blood donation program in AC Bus from Blood Bank at Debagram Police R.O.P with 53 donors.

28/01/2021

Thalassemia Detection Camp

Conducted thalassemia detection camp at Bethuadahari College for community health screening.

22/04/2021

Bio Medical Waste Management

Managed Bio Medical Waste during Bidhansava Election across 15 Gram Panchayats of Kaliganj Block.

14/05/2021

Oxygen Parlor Establishment

Started oxygen parlor with 2 Oxygen Concentrators and 2 Handy Oxygen Cylinders, operated for 2 months.

03/11/2021

Free Medical Check-up Program

Organized comprehensive medical check-up at Kaliganj Satyacharan Girls High School with Nivedita Nursing Home, serving 170 people with Blood Sugar and Echocardiography tests.

Acknowledgment

On behalf of Kaliganj Block Committee, we extend our heartfelt gratitude and respect to all members of the guiding committee, board members, volunteers, local people who love Red Cross, and all respected local residents who supported our COVID-19 response efforts.

Together, we demonstrated the power of community solidarity and humanitarian spirit during one of the most challenging periods in recent history.
