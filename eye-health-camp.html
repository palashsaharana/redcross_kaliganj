About Eye & Health Camp Program

Our Eye & Health Camp program provides comprehensive eye care services and general health screening to underserved communities. We organize regular camps to ensure early detection and treatment of eye diseases and other health conditions.

Eye Examination

Complete eye check-ups and vision screening

Medicine Distribution

Free medicines for common ailments and conditions
