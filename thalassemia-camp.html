Thalassemia Camp

Building a Thalassemia-free community through awareness and screening

About Thalassemia Camp

Our Thalassemia identification and awareness camps are crucial in our mission to build a Thalassemia-free India. Through early detection, genetic counseling, and community education, we work to prevent the transmission of this genetic disorder and support affected families.
