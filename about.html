About Indian Red Cross Society Kaliganj Block Branch

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to serving humanity

Our Mission

To prevent and alleviate human suffering wherever it may be found, to protect life and health, and to ensure respect for human dignity, particularly in times of armed conflict and other emergencies.

Our Vision

To be the leading humanitarian organization in Kaliganj, inspiring hope, building resilience, and creating lasting change in our community through compassionate service.

Our Goals & Objectives

Our goal is to protect public health, education, and the environment for the welfare of society. In our district, our main objective is to raise greater awareness among people to prevent social issues such as child marriage and human trafficking.

We are committed to building a Thalassemia-free India through Thalassemia identification camps. Through blood donation camps, we help support blood banks with essential services.

In addition, organizing eye and health check-up camps and standing beside people in any emergency situation are our sole objectives.

Our History

The Indian Red Cross Society Kaliganj Block Branch was established with the noble mission of serving humanity in our local community. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing humanitarian aid, disaster relief, and community health services to the people of Kaliganj and surrounding areas.

Our branch operates under the principles of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement, guided by the fundamental principles of humanity, impartiality, neutrality, independence, voluntary service, unity, and universality.

Over the years, we have grown from a small group of dedicated volunteers to a well-organized branch with multiple programs and services that address the diverse needs of our community.

Fundamental Principles

The seven principles that guide all our humanitarian work

Humanity

Preventing and alleviating human suffering wherever it may be found

Impartiality

Making no discrimination as to nationality, race, religious beliefs, class or political opinions

Neutrality

Not taking sides in hostilities or engaging in controversies of a political nature

Independence

Maintaining autonomy so we can act in accordance with Red Cross principles

Voluntary Service

A voluntary relief movement not prompted in any manner by desire for gain

Unity

There can be only one Red Cross Society in any one country

Universality

The Red Cross is a worldwide institution with equal status and responsibilities
